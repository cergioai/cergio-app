═══════════════ (b) WhatsApp nudge channel ═══════════════

  WhatsApp deep-link: https://wa.me/{e164}?text={msg}
  SMS deep-link:      sms:{phone}?body={msg}

  Both pre-fill the canonical nudge copy:

    "Hi {first} — I'm on Cergio. Each friend who joins + books
     earns me $250. Want to try? {tracked-invite-url}"

  The tracked URL is buildInviteUrl(my-id) so the friend's eventual
  signup + first booking credit the inviter (Tarik 2026-06-04: "once
  they convert we'll notify via the converting medium — sms or wasp —
  in addition to notifications").

  WhatsApp is the PRIMARY button (Tarik: "far more effective for
  users"). SMS stays as the fallback in the same row.

═══════════════ (c) PublicProfileScreen "By the numbers" block ═══════════════

  New stats card under the role + Connector badges:

    [Invited 23]  [Joined 12]  [Booked 6]
    [Reco'd 18]   [Services 3]

  Cells render only when value > 0. On the SELF view, the Invited /
  Joined cells link to /earnings/invites and Booked links to
  /earnings — turning the numbers into action.

  Sub-line spells the cut:
    "You earn 7% of every friend's booking up to $250 per friend,
     plus $12.50 chain bonus per friend-of-friend."

  Block hides silently when all five counts = 0 so empty profiles
  don't look broken.

  New API: getPublicProfileStats(profileId) — invited / joined /
  booked / recommended / listedServices counts. $ amounts stay
  private to the self-view (EarningsScreen).
