Frame 3: PAID state + flexible-time accept + per-service reco's received

- PAID state (request with a budget / non-Connector): headline shows the
  service "· up to $X" instead of the free-spotlight exchange; the message
  reads "…My budget is up to $X — what's your price?"; the spotlight
  placeholder + free-marketing benefit are hidden; the primary action becomes
  "Place a bid" → a composer with a price + optional note via
  respondToRequest('offered', priceCents, message).
- Flexible-time accept: when the request has no specific start time, the
  primary action is "Pick a time & accept" → a datetime picker; the chosen
  time is sent as "Proposed time: …" in the response. Specific-time requests
  keep the one-tap accept.
- Counter (free only) and Decline retained; one unified composer drives bid /
  counter / pick-a-time.
- Services now show "(N recos received)" next to each.
