Inbox: "Book a time" on an accepted offer — closes the barter loop end-to-end

The two-step barter stalled because the requester had no direct way to turn a provider's
offer into a scheduled booking (the response card only linked to the profile), so no booking
existed for the provider to mark complete. Now the "Responses to your requests" card has a
"Book a free time →" / "Book a time · $X" button that runs handleBook (free-barter gate →
ScheduleSheet → createBooking), creating the dated booking that flows to Upcoming for both
parties and lets the provider proceed (confirm → mark complete → rate → IG post → confirm).
