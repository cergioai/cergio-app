Reverse spotlight: inbound card leads with provider service reputation

When a Connector views a provider's spotlight request, the card now leads with
the provider's SERVICE reputation — their services each with reco's received,
plus their bio — since that's how a Connector decides whether to offer a
spotlight (not the provider's IG reach). listMyInboundSpotlightRequests now
hydrates each provider's bio + services + reco's-received (batched).
