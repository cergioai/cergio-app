timestamp) and inserts swallowed errors via `-s` + `>>$LOG`. Three fixes:

  (a) Cleanup block at the TOP — deletes prior testflow personas, their
      services/offerings, recommendations, network edges, connector
      applications, and prior seed-marker earnings (meta @> '{seed:true}')
      BEFORE inserting fresh ones. Idempotent.

      silently rolling back the whole transaction).

      removed.
  (d) Verification block at the END — queries actual row counts after
      writes and prints them with expected values. If recs_on_penny=0
      after a run, you know the inserts failed BEFORE looking elsewhere.
