Sample Mockup Tokens.command (new, root)
  Drag a PNG onto the script — get back:
    • Top 15 colors by area frequency (brand palette)
    • Spot samples at 5 hard-coded coordinates (edit per mockup)
    • Vertical scan column-by-column listing color transitions
      (reveals divider heights + section bands)
    • Horizontal scan to confirm left content margin
  Tarik never has to eyeball a hex again.

Audit Spotlights.command (new, root)
  Runs the spotlight-flow audit Tarik asked for separately. Reports:
    • Status histogram (where rows pile up in the funnel)

      cleared, paid > 7d not posted, declined w/ earnings)
