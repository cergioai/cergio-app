fix(notify): SMS send for A2P 10DLC + claim-email referral copy

Item 2 (reco sent no text) — root cause + fix:
- notify-user only sent SMS via TWILIO_FROM_NUMBER (a plain long code). A2P
  10DLC requires the Messaging Service SID, and the deployed secret may be named
  TWILIO_FROM / TWILIO_MESSAGING_SERVICE_SID. Now: prefer MessagingServiceSid
  (TWILIO_MESSAGING_SERVICE_SID | TWILIO_MESSAGING_SID), fall back to a From
  number (TWILIO_FROM_NUMBER | TWILIO_FROM). Can't be silently disabled by an
  env-name mismatch.
- The skip/error result now reports WHICH secret is missing and surfaces
  Twilio's error body, so a PENDING 10DLC campaign is diagnosable from the
  function response instead of a silent no-op.
  NOTE: actual delivery still requires the 10DLC campaign to be APPROVED at
  Twilio (it was PENDING vetting) and the From/Service registered to it.
