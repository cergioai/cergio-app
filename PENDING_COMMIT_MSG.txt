Freeze SPEC-49d: "recos made" intentionally exceeds Go-Tos shown

No app behavior change — documents + protects existing behavior (Tarik
2026-06-17). A recommendation made to a not-yet-registered provider
stores the review + their contact so they can be notified to register;
it COUNTS toward "recos made" and drives the notify-to-register loop,
but is NOT displayed on the profile (no claimed provider/photo). Go-Tos
renders only recos whose service_id resolves. The count/section
difference is intentional — do not reconcile them.

Also adds scripts/diag_profile_recos.mjs: a read-only diagnostic that
reports a profile's recos-made vs how many resolve to a service.
