1. 7%/10% TRUTHFULNESS — copy now matches the real economics

EarnExplainerScreen had three places saying "Cergio earns a 7% fee" or
"Cergio's 7% fee on that booking is $21". Wrong. Cergio's actual fee
is 10%; of that, 7% flows to the inviter/recommender. Old copy
conflated the two — misleading.

Fixed shape (uses REWARDS.platformFeePercent + referrerSharePercent
+ friendCapWindowMonths — no hardcoded percentages anywhere):

  Old:   "Cergio earns a 7% fee — and we share that with you, up to $250"
  New:   "Cergio's platform fee is 10% on every booking. When you invite
          or recommend, we share 7% of each booking with you — up to $250
          per friend."

  Old:   "Your friend Jamie books a $300 deep clean. Cergio's 7% fee on
          that booking is $21 — and you earn that."
  New:   "Jamie books a $300 deep clean. Cergio's 10% fee on that booking
          is $30. You earn 7% = $21 as Jamie's inviter. Keep going across
          Jamie's future bookings until you've earned the full $250."

The example computes both numbers from REWARDS so they can never drift.
