Spotlight request = identical flow to a free-service request: broadcast + "we'll notify you" + cancel

- broadcastSpotlightRequest() in api.js: provider-side mirror of createRequestAndNotify.
  Fans out one pending spotlight_requests row per matching Connector (free swap,
  official_price_cents=0), skips Connectors who already have a live request,
  fires notify-spotlight best-effort. Connectors see it inbound on /spotlight/:id.
- BrowseConnectorsScreen: replaced the dead-end "List a service to start" pick-a-
  Connector roster entry with the same paradigm as the consumer service request:
    1. Entry: "Ask Connectors to spotlight you" -> Request spotlights for {service}
       (one button per listed service; "List a service first" only when none).
    2. Waiting: "We'll notify you when Connectors respond" + inline Cancel
       (mirror of the consumer's "we'll let you know when offers land").
    3. Responses: confirmed Connectors surface in the roster (offered/countered/accepted).
- Fixes the bug where a provider with services was told to "List a service."
