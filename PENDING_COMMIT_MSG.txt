  Title: "Earn $250 per friend who books."

2. SCENE 2 — fan-out replaces the 3-person chain

Old: simple You → Jamie → Alex chain with two coin arrows. Read as
"one friend-of-friend, +$12.50, done." Underweighted the actual

influencers" instantly.

  Coin pill: "+$12.50 × many"
  Title: "Friends invite friends — chain grows fast."
  Side-callout (top-right): "Influencer chain: 1 → 10 → 100+"
