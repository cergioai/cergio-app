═══════════════ 1. CODE — creditChainOnFirstBooking ═══════════════

`src/lib/referral.js`:
  + `creditChainOnFirstBooking({ invite, consumerId, bookingId })`:
    walks one hop up from the direct invite row, writes the
    grandparent inviter a tier='fof' earnings row at $12.50
    (= REWARDS.friendOfFriendBonus). Cap at depth 2 (great-
    grandparent does NOT earn).  Idempotency check on
    (profile_id, kind, booking_id, tier='fof') so re-firing the
    credit doesn't double-pay. Self-loop guard.
  + `creditInviterOnFirstBooking` now stamps meta.tier='direct'
    on the direct row + fire-and-forget calls the chain credit
    after the direct write. Failure in chain credit does NOT
    affect direct credit.

Schema is unchanged; the new code path uses existing earnings
columns + a `meta.tier` field already wired into EarningsScreen's
"Direct" vs "Chain +5%" pill render.
