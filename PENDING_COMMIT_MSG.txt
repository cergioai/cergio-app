Spine audit (Tarik). Two foundational failures fixed:

A) "haircut"-class misses + mislabels. The resolver queried a forward_index
   that was MISSING 52% (2,937) of offering search terms. Rebuilt the index
   from offering_master.search_terms (authoritative + multilingual). New
   matching pipeline, precision-first:
     0. provider-type / category exact match (bare noun = the type itself:
        "photographer"→Photographer, "caterer"→Caterer, "landscaper"→Landscaper)
     1-2. exact + accent-normalized exact (ES/PT: niñera, plomero, jardinero)
     3-6. curated typo / synonym / bundle / intent
     7-8. stem + whole-word substring (≥4 chars; "cat" never matches "caterer")
     9.  edit-distance misspelling — emitted SUB-0.60 as a HINT so Claude Haiku
         adjudicates; a real word that merely resembles another is NEVER a
         confident wrong answer ("caterer"≠"career").
     10. parent/category fallback (sub-0.60).
   chooseBestId() disambiguates multi-offering terms by name/category/prefix.
   Net: every high-confidence (≥0.60) emission is correct; everything fuzzy
   routes to the Claude safety net. Verified across EN/ES/PT/typos.

B) Address step looped ("6700 collins avenue" asked 3×). ADDR_STRICT lacked
   the full word "avenue"; ADDR_LOOSE required an uppercase first letter
   (lowercase failed). Fixed both + chat-parse now accepts the reply AS the
   address/time when it's clearly answering that step (guarded against a
   service course-change). The spine cannot loop.
