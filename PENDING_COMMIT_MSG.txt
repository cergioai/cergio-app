Inbox "Accept" now does accept-with-time → confirmed booking too (SPEC-47h)

You were accepting from the inbox card's Accept button, which still sent a quick offer
("waiting on them to pick a time"). Now the inbox Accept opens an inline time picker and
confirms the booking via accept_request_with_time — same as the detail screen. Pick a time
(or leave blank), tap Confirm booking → it's a confirmed Upcoming job for both, reschedulable.
